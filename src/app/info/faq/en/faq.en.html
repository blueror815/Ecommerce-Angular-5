Payments

What payment methods are available?

Payments are processed by PayPal. On PayPal you can pay with a credit/debit card as well.

Which currency can I pay in?

You can pay in US dollars, euros, Japanese yens and Russian rubles.

Which currency to prefer?

We recommend to avoid currency conversions, if possible. If you have a dollar account, pay in dollars. If you have a euro account pay in euros and so on.

How long does it take to process a payment?

Usually payments are processed within 1-5 minutes.

Shipping

How much is shipping?

Shipping is included in item prices.

When will you ship my items?

Most items are shipped within a week after payment.

How long does delivery take?

It depends on where you live, but most packages are delivered within 1-3 weeks.

Do packages have tracking numbers?

Yes, all packages are shipped with tracking numbers.

Do I need to pay custom duties?

Duties and taxes in your country are not included in the item price. If the package value exceeds the duty-free limit, you will need to pay duties. We recommend you to check in advance rules for calculating duties and taxes in your country.

Are packages insured?

Yes, all packages are insured for their full value.

Will it be delivered to my doorstep?

Usually, but not always. We recommend to watch the tracking of your package and contact the postal service yourself if necessary.
